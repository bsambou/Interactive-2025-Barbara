Landscapes Through the Lens

BBC Earth’s photography offers a breathtaking glimpse into Africa through the eyes of its wildlife. From wide, reflective waters where elephants bathe, to soaring mountain ranges wrapped in mist and mystery, each image captures the land’s raw beauty. In the deserts, golden dunes stretch endlessly, alive with resilient creatures navigating the heat and dust. These perspectives reveal not just landscapes, but the daily journeys of animals within them—hunting, surviving, thriving. Through this lens, we don’t just observe nature—we step into it, experiencing Africa’s diverse terrains as if we were part of the wild itself, seeing as its animals do.
