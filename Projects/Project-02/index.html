Landscapes Through the Lens

Intro
BBC Earth’s photography offers a breathtaking glimpse into Africa through the eyes of its wildlife. From reflective waters where elephants bathe, to soaring mountain ranges wrapped in mist, each image captures the land’s raw beauty. In the deserts, golden dunes stretch endlessly, alive with resilient creatures navigating the heat and dust. These perspectives reveal not just landscapes, but the daily journeys of animals within them — hunting, surviving and decompressing. Through this lens, you won’t just observe nature — you will step into it, experiencing diverse terrains as if you are part of the wild itself, seeing it as animals do.
